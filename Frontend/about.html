About SQE-Easy

Learn about our mission and the team behind SQE-Easy

Our Story

SQE-Easy was founded in 2022 by a team of legal professionals and educational technologists with a shared vision: to make SQE preparation accessible, effective, and engaging for all aspiring solicitors in the UK.

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with expert legal knowledge to provide a comprehensive, adaptive learning experience tailored to each student's needs.

Our Mission

At SQE-Easy, our mission is to empower the next generation of legal professionals by providing the most effective and user-friendly SQE preparation tools available. We strive to:

  • Offer a comprehensive and up-to-date question bank covering all SQE topics
  • Provide personalized learning experiences through adaptive technology
  • Support students with detailed performance analytics and study recommendations
  • Make high-quality SQE preparation accessible to all, regardless of background
